Pairing Matrix Reloaded

Having created and used pairing matrix visualization creator for the quite sometime, Based on the observations and feedback I did some enhancements

  • Coloring of contributor icons (circles)
  • Positioning any contributor icon around the ring by click and drag
  • Merging duplicate contributor names by click, drag and drop over another icon

Happy visualizing!
